Adugo — Jaguar and Dogs

Playable online • Human vs Human • Human vs Computer

Adugo Arena

Asymmetrical hunt game on a line-based graph board.

Jaguar starts first

Jaguar

Mobile hunter. Capture dogs by jumping.

Dogs

Pack defenders. Trap the jaguar.

Capture objective

Remaining captures: 14

  • Board is a graph: pieces move only along connected lines.
  • Jaguar moves one step, or captures by jumping over an adjacent dog to an empty connected landing node.
  • Dogs cannot capture; they win by trapping the jaguar so it has no legal moves.
  • Jaguar continues a capture chain in the same turn when additional captures are available.
  • Current variant: jaguar wins by capturing all 14 dogs.
  1. Select one of your pieces (matching the current turn).
  2. Legal destinations glow in blue (capture landings in red).
  3. In Human vs Computer mode, input is locked while the AI is thinking.
  4. Undo in Human vs Computer mode reverts one full pair (human + AI) when possible.

Jaguar: maximize mobility, keep central lanes open, and chain captures when possible.

Dogs: create a compact net, deny escape lanes, and avoid isolated dogs that can be jumped.
