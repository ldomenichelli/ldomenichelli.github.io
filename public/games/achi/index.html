Match flow

8 placements remaining

  • The board has 9 intersections connected by horizontal, vertical, and diagonal lines.
  • Each player has 4 pieces and wins by forming an uninterrupted line of 3 of their own pieces.
  • During placement, players alternate placing on any empty intersection. A line of 3 wins immediately.
  • Movement begins only after all 8 pieces have been placed.
  • During movement, pieces slide one step along a connected line to an adjacent empty intersection. No jumping.
  1. In placement, click any highlighted empty intersection to place your next piece.
  2. In movement, click one of your own pieces to see legal adjacent destinations.
  3. Selected pieces glow; legal destinations pulse softly.
  4. In Human vs Computer mode, input is locked while the AI is thinking.
  5. Undo in Human vs Computer mode rewinds to the most recent human-to-move state when possible.

Placement: contest the center early and avoid handing the opponent an immediate line on move 3.

Movement: look for dual threats that force one reply while your other line stays alive.
