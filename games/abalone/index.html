Abalone

A polished duel on a radius-4 hex board ( cells).

2 playersAbstract strategyQuick duel

How it works

  • On your turn, do one action with 1–3 of your own aligned marbles.
  • Move one space in any of the 6 hex directions.
  • Inline: move along the line orientation.
  • Broadside: shift sideways while keeping the line orientation.
  • Sumito push (inline only): legal only with strictly more marbles (2v1, 3v1, 3v2). Equal strength cannot push.
  • A push needs empty space behind opponents, unless that edge is off-board (then a marble is ejected).
  • First player to eject six opposing marbles wins.

    Short strategy notes

    • Control center lanes to avoid edge ejections.
    • Build 3-marble spines; they project both threat and defense.
    • Prefer forcing turns where your opponent can only react.
    • Keep groups connected—isolated marbles become leverage points.
